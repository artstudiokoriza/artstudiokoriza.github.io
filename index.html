Rules of tattoo care during the healing period.

1. After 4 to 8 hours after applying the tattoo, remove the protective cloth.
2. Wash the place with fresh tattoo warm water with antibacterial soap
3. Blot the tattoo with a paper napkin and allow to dry before applying the cream, so as not to create a "bath effect" and not to steam.
4. Apply a thin layer of cream(ointment) "Bepanten Plus". The cream is easily absorbed, the cream leaves a greasy mark, but good on the bends.
During the entire healing period 5-10 days:

• Prevent skin from drying out. The tattoo should always be in a dry elastic state. For that you perform every day 2-4 times a day the recommendations listed above, from 2 to 4 points.
Plastic wrap, band-AIDS, bandages - do not use!!! otherwise, you tired of a tattoo.

• After tattooing, it is recommended to wear spacious clothes, excluding the contact of matter with the tattooed surface of the skin, to avoid skin irritation.

• To eliminate the Solarium, the sauna, steam room and pool.

• Do not expose yourself to heavy physical activity (sports training, hard physical work).

• Do not scratch the tattoo. Do not tear off the crust, as this can lead to the formation of scars.

• Do not use for treatment alcohol-containing drugs and medicines not specified in the recommendations.

• A couple of days after tattooing, the pattern is covered with a crust, which remains until complete healing. The healing period is usually 7-10 days.

• Sometimes, after the healing of the tattooed area of the skin, it is necessary to correct the pattern, since there are gaps in the image during healing. Tattoo correction in our salon is carried out free of charge - the price is calculated immediately taking into account the consumables for this.

• Do not expose the tattooed area of the skin to direct sunlight until it is completely healed. Ultraviolet light has a negative impact on the skin and pigments.
To prolong the "life" of your tattoo - it is necessary to protect it with a cream with a high protection factor (40-50 SPF)

And remember, any tattoo must be updated over time, but when - after 5 or 10 years - depends solely on YOU(care, according to the recommendations) and your body.
